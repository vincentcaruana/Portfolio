"PLAY" Clothing Collection

Industrial Design, Branding, Sewing, Pattern Drafting

This clothing collection is an exploration of nostalgia and boyhood. These garments intentionally disrupt traditional silhouettes and ideas to create a recognizable yet unique aesthetic. Details such as the cartoonishly oversized aglets and the playful carousel pattern serve to capture the elusive feeling of a childhood memory.

I controled every piece of production, managing all aspects of manufacturing, sample development, and branding. For both comfort and quality, the "Detached Hood" and T-shirt are constructed from 100% cotton, while the accompanying cargo shorts utilize a durable cotton canvas material.
