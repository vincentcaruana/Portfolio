Paranoia Animation

Motion Design, Adobe After Effects, Illustration, Adobe Illustrator

This animation was intended to visually depict the experience of paranoia, set to music by Galen Tipton. I used this project to experiment with motion in abstract spaces and the pairing of movement to music. My goal was to make an experience that felt both satisfying and impactful.
